About Paya Street

Paya Street Coffee is a small-scale, community-centered coffee shop that opened its Angeles City branch between mid-2023 and 2024. Known for its minimalist interior and inviting atmosphere, Paya Street was founded by Lianna, Marty, and Lester Wayne Evangelista. Their vision? To make premium-quality drinks more a ccessible and enjoyable for everyone—especially students and young professionals.

More Than a Cup

At Paya Street, we don’t just serve coffee — we build community. Whether you’re sipping your go-to latte or wearing your favorite tee, Paya Street is all about comfort, creativity, and connection. Our merch line — from cozy shirts to minimalist caps and playful stickers — is designed to extend the Paya experience beyond the café. It’s coffee culture you can wear, rep, and share. Purpose meets style. Wherever you are, take a piece of Paya with you.

Effortless Style - Intentional Living

Every item in our collection is inspired by our space — minimalist, welcoming, and made for moments. We believe style should feel just as good as your first sip in the morning: effortless and intentional. By wearing Paya, you're repping more than a coffee shop. You’re supporting a local dream, a student-friendly space, and a movement that celebrates affordable quality and authentic community.
