ABOUT PAYA STREET

Paya Street Café is your cozy retreat in the heart of Angeles City, where every cup of coffee is crafted with care and every bite is made to delight. Whether you're looking for a morning pick-me-up, a relaxing afternoon break, or a spot to catch up with friends, our café offers the perfect mix of comfort, warmth, and flavor. With a menu featuring specialty coffees, fresh pastries, and savory snacks, paired with a welcoming ambiance and soft music, Paya Street Café is more than just a place to eat and drink—it’s a place to feel at home.
